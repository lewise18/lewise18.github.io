Best EMail Provider for 2021

By Lewis Eaton, 22/05/21

I'm going to say it. GMail is no longer number 1 on the EMail provider list. mail.com is becoming very popular nowadays, they have a free plan and a paid plan with no advertisments. mail.com allows you to select a domain from a list and use your own domain. mail.com also allows you to create EMail aliases so that you don't need to give others your main EMail, you can give them a seperate one. It's handy if multiple people use the same account because you can add the alises and create automations depending on what alias that an EMail gets sent to. You need to be 16 to create a mail.com email account.
